VSM Aerospace AME Training School is a DGCA Approved institute and one of the best AME training institutions in India. With over 30 years of excellence, we have built a strong foundation in aircraft maintenance engineering education with a proud alumni of 2500+ students working globally.

Training Facilities

Aircraft Hangar

Well-equipped hangar with HS-748, light aircraft, and helicopters for practical training on real aircraft

Jet Engine Lab

Advanced jet engine laboratory with real turbine engines and associated ground equipment for hands-on learning

Avionics Lab

Electrical Lab

Machine Shop
